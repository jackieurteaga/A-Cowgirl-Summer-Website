Jackie, Ashley, Vale + Carlos are heading to Glacier Thursday morning. Pick your level — all are jaw-dropping. Logan Pass opens ~late June, confirm road status before going.

Moderate · 9.6 mi RT · Flexible Turn-Back
Iceberg Lake
Best Turquoise Water
One of Glacier's most jaw-dropping destinations — a high alpine lake famous for its vivid turquoise color and floating icebergs even in September. The trail is surprisingly gradual (1,200 ft gain over 4.8 mi). Can turn back at the cirque opening for a shorter version — still spectacular views of the cliffs and peaks.
Many Glacier area · ~1 hr 20 min from Whitefish
4–5 hrs full loop · shorter if you turn back early
1,275 ft elevation gain · gradual climb
Trail Info →
Easy–Moderate · Choose Your Distance
Gunsight Lake / Florence Falls
Go At Your Own Pace
A choose-your-own-adventure trail off the Going-to-the-Sun Road. Hike 4 miles to Florence Falls for a quick and rewarding waterfall stop, or continue to stunning glacier-fed Gunsight Lake at mile 6 — brilliant turquoise water at the base of dramatic peaks. Turn back whenever you're ready.
Jackson Glacier Overlook trailhead · on Going-to-the-Sun Rd
Florence Falls: ~4 hrs RT · Gunsight Lake: ~6 hrs RT
1,275 ft elevation gain to lake · no permit needed
